Tumor Response

From the graph above we can observe that Capomulim had the best effective response than the rest of the observable tratments. During the pass of the days it reduce meaningfully the tumor volume in the mice.

The rest of the observable treatments showed that the tumor increased its volume, but Ketapril showed the the worst response of those treatments, even showing an apparently increased behavior during the last 5 days.
